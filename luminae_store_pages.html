We believe the ordinary can be extraordinary

Luminae was born from a simple but powerful idea — that your daily rituals deserve to feel as intentional as the life you're building. The soap you reach for every morning. The candle you light when the day finally quiets down. The body oil you smooth on before bed. These aren't just products. They're moments. And moments, when nurtured well, become a life well-lived.

We create premium, natural home wellness and body care products for women who believe that slowing down is not a luxury — it's a practice. Everything we make is thoughtfully crafted using clean, natural ingredients, with no compromises on quality, sustainability, or your skin's wellbeing.

Our story

Luminae started in a small kitchen in 2022, with a batch of handmade soap, a notebook full of scent blends, and a belief that women deserved better than synthetic, overcomplicated products wrapped in empty promises.

Our founder spent years searching for natural body care that actually worked — that smelled beautiful, felt luxurious, and contained nothing she couldn't pronounce. When she couldn't find it, she made it. She shared it with friends. Friends shared it with theirs. And slowly, quietly, Luminae grew.

Today, every product is still made in small batches, with the same careful attention to ingredients, scent, and sensory experience that started it all. We're proud to be a brand that has never compromised on what matters.

Natural Ingredients

Every formula starts with nature — cold-pressed oils, botanical extracts, pure essential oils. Nothing synthetic, nothing unnecessary.

Small Batch Crafted

We make in small quantities so every product is fresh, attentive, and held to the highest standards before it reaches you.

Sustainable Packaging

Kraft boxes, glass vessels, recycled paper. We're committed to packaging that's as kind to the planet as our formulas are to your skin.

Cruelty-Free Always

Not tested on animals. Not ever. We believe beauty should never come at another creature's expense.

What we make

The Luminae collection spans three thoughtfully curated categories. Our Body Rituals range includes artisan soaps, nourishing body oils, and exfoliating scrubs for a complete, sensory bathing experience. Our Home Ambiance collection — hand-poured soy candles and botanical reed diffusers — brings warmth and fragrance to your space. And our Ritual Bundles bring both worlds together in beautifully packaged gift sets, perfect for gifting or self-treating.

Our promise to you

Every product we make is free from parabens, sulphates, synthetic dyes, and artificial fragrance. We use only ingredients we'd be happy for our own family to use. And we back everything with a satisfaction guarantee — because we believe you should love what you bring into your home.

Join the ritual

Luminae isn't just a brand. It's an invitation to slow down, to notice, to treat yourself with the same warmth you give so freely to everyone else. We're glad you found us. Now, let's build a ritual worth keeping.

With warmth,
The Luminae Team

Have a question about Luminae products, orders, or ingredients? We've answered the most common ones below. Can't find what you're looking for? Reach us at hello@luminae.in — we're always happy to help.

Products & Ingredients

Are Luminae products truly natural and free from harsh chemicals?
Yes, absolutely. Every Luminae product is formulated with natural ingredients — cold-pressed oils, botanical extracts, himalayan minerals, and pure essential oils. We never use parabens, sulphates, synthetic dyes, or artificial fragrance. The full ingredient list is listed on every product page so you always know exactly what you're putting on your skin or in your home.
Are your products suitable for sensitive skin?
Most of our products are formulated to be gentle enough for sensitive skin, including our Goat Milk & Honey Artisan Soap Bar, which is especially kind to sensitive or dry skin types. However, we always recommend doing a patch test on a small area before using any new product, particularly if you have known allergies or very reactive skin. If you have specific concerns, feel free to email us and we can recommend the best products for your skin type.
Are Luminae products cruelty-free and vegan?
All Luminae products are 100% cruelty-free — we never test on animals and never will. Most of our range is also vegan-friendly. Our Goat Milk Soap Bar uses goat milk (an animal-derived ingredient), so it is not vegan, but it is cruelty-free. All other products in our range are fully vegan. Product pages clearly indicate where applicable.
How long do the candles and diffusers last?
Our Soy Wax Ritual Candles provide up to 45 hours of burn time when used correctly — always trim the wick to 5mm before each burn and allow the wax to pool to the edges on the first use. Our Botanical Reed Diffusers last up to 60 days depending on room size and ventilation. Flipping the reeds every 3–4 days helps maintain consistent fragrance intensity.

Orders & Delivery

How long will my order take to arrive?
We process all orders within 1–2 business days. Standard delivery across India takes 4–7 business days. Express delivery (2–3 business days) is available at checkout for an additional charge. You'll receive a tracking link by email as soon as your order is dispatched. For metro cities, delivery is typically faster — often 2–4 business days.
Do you offer free shipping?
Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over ₹999. Orders below ₹999 are charged a flat shipping fee of ₹79. Express shipping is available at checkout for ₹149 regardless of order value. Free shipping is automatically applied at checkout once your cart reaches the qualifying amount.
Can I return or exchange a product?
We want you to love your Luminae purchase. If you're not completely satisfied, we accept returns within 14 days of delivery for unopened, unused products in their original packaging. Due to the personal nature of body care products, we're unable to accept returns on opened or used items unless they are defective or damaged on arrival. To initiate a return, please email hello@luminae.in with your order number and reason for return.
My order arrived damaged. What should I do?
We're so sorry to hear that — this is rare but occasionally happens during transit. Please email us at hello@luminae.in within 48 hours of delivery with your order number and a photo of the damaged item. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund right away at no cost to you. Your satisfaction is our top priority and we'll make it right, no questions asked.

Still have a question?

We'd love to hear from you. Email us at hello@luminae.in or fill in the form on our Contact Us page and we'll get back to you within one business day.

Packaging

All Luminae products are carefully packed in recyclable kraft paper and bubble-lined mailers to ensure they arrive safely. Gift sets and bundles are shipped in their original premium gift boxes, nested inside a protective outer box. We care about our environmental footprint — our packaging is 90% plastic-free.

Returns Policy

Eligibility for Returns

We accept returns within 14 days of the delivery date under the following conditions:

  • The product is unused and unopened
  • The product is in its original packaging with all seals intact
  • You have proof of purchase (order confirmation email)

Due to the personal care nature of our products, we are unable to accept returns on opened, used, or tampered items unless they are defective or damaged on arrival.

Non-Returnable Items

  • Opened body care products (soaps, oils, scrubs)
  • Lit candles or opened diffusers
  • Personalised or custom orders
  • Products purchased during clearance or final sale

How to Initiate a Return

  1. Email us at hello@luminae.in with your order number and reason for return
  2. We'll respond within 1 business day with a return authorisation and instructions
  3. Pack the item securely in its original packaging and ship to the address provided
  4. Share the return tracking number with us once dispatched

Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the return is due to a defective or incorrect item.

Refunds

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5–7 business days. Refunds are issued to the original payment method. You'll receive an email confirmation once your refund has been processed. Please note that it may take an additional 3–5 business days for the refund to appear in your account depending on your bank or payment provider.

Damaged or Incorrect Orders

If your order arrives damaged, defective, or incorrect, please email hello@luminae.in within 48 hours of delivery with your order number and a photo of the issue. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund immediately — no return required for damaged items.

Exchanges

We don't offer direct exchanges at this time. If you'd like a different product, please return the original item (subject to our returns policy) and place a new order. This ensures the fastest turnaround for you.
